Da Vinci Surgical System (medical robot)

The Da Vinci Surgical System is a sophisticated robotic platform designed to assist surgeons in performing minimally invasive surgery. It consists of robotic arms with surgical instruments and a high-definition 3D camera. Surgeons control the system from a console and can perform precise movements with enhanced dexterity, enabling complex procedures with smaller incisions, reduced scarring, and faster recovery for patients.

Robonaut (NASA's humanoid robot)

Robonaut is a humanoid robot developed by NASA and General Motors. It was designed to work alongside astronauts on the International Space Station (ISS) and perform tasks that are dangerous or repetitive. Robonaut has a humanoid upper body with dexterous hands, which allows it to handle tools and perform delicate tasks. Its development aims to explore how robots can assist humans in space exploration and other hazardous environments.

Sophia (humanoid robot)

Sophia is a humanoid robot developed by Hanson Robotics. It is known for its advanced artificial intelligence and expressive facial features, making it appear remarkably human-like. Sophia has the ability to process speech, recognize faces, and engage in conversations with people. It has been showcased at various events worldwide and has sparked discussions about the potential future of AI and human-robot interaction.
